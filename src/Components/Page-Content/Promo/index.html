Promos

Promo boxes!

Show off your products

With promo boxes you can easily distinguish your product form the site and get more users attention. And it costs you only a few clicks!

Promo is sometimes referred to as Call To Action, so they need a link and shall shout to the user: click me!

Easily drag and drop components to the page. It is like Lego, only for grown-ups.
Drag And Drop Editing
Change the way the component looks like with just a single selection. Display different fields by selecting one of the available variants.
Rendering Variants






You can even specify an image as background. Just try it and you cannot stop!




 

Promos can be made into a component that is extended by authors!

There is no better way of showing your call to action than using a promo box with a nice image. Courtesy of rendering variants you can design any configuration you can imagine and have embedded placeholders to place any other component into your call to actions!

With such flexibility the opportunities are limitless! Take a look at the examples below.
